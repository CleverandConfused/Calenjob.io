How It Works

Schedule with real geography in mind.

CalenJob fits into how you already plan your day. Just add the address and let the app handle the rest.

Create a calendar event tied to a real address. Or send a customer your public booking link and let them pick a slot that fits your day.

Features

Everything a contractor calendar needs. Nothing it doesn't.

CalenJob is not field service management software. It is a calendar that actually works for people who drive to every job.

Automatic drive time

Every job gets a calculated commute from the previous stop, using live traffic data. Add your own buffer on top for loading, parking, or just breathing room.

Smart departure alerts

Push notifications remind you when it is time to leave for the next job. Stop running late, stop apologizing to customers.

Mileage and cost tracking

Every trip is logged with distance and your own custom per-mile rate. See daily, monthly, and yearly totals for tax deductions and client billing.

Customer booking

Send customers a link to your public booking page. When they pick a time, CalenJob factors in the drive from your previous job. Your day stays honest even when customers are the ones adding the appointment.

Interactive route map

See your whole day plotted on a map with the drive route drawn between stops. Switch between standard, satellite, and hybrid views.

Recurring schedules

Weekly maintenance clients, standing appointments, and regular routes build themselves. Set it once, and CalenJob fills in the calendar.
