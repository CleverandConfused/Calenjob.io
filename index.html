How It Works

Schedule with real geography in mind.

CalenJob fits into how you already plan your day. Just add the address and let the app handle the rest.

1

Add a job with an address

Create a calendar event tied to a real location. Residential, commercial, wherever the work is.

2

Drive time fills in automatically

Features

Everything a contractor calendar needs. Nothing it doesn't.

CalenJob is not field service management software. It is a calendar that actually works for people who drive to every job.

Automatic drive time

Every event gets a calculated commute from the previous stop. Add your own buffer on top for loading, parking, or just breathing room.

Area blocks

Reserve a day for a specific part of town. If you try to book something outside that zone, CalenJob warns you before your schedule gets scattered.

Mileage and cost tracking

Every trip is logged with distance and a custom per-mile rate. See daily, monthly, and yearly totals for taxes and business planning.

Multi-calendar views

See your schedule alongside crew members or other contractors. Great for small teams that need visibility without complicated software.

Map-based reordering

View all your stops on a map and rearrange them visually. You see the geography, you make the call. No black-box routing required.

Recurring events and blocks

Set up weekly maintenance clients, lunch breaks, admin time, or anything else that repeats. CalenJob builds around them.
